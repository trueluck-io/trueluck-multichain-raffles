## How trueluck Multichain Raffles Work

A major innovation behind trueluck is its multichain infrastructure. Rather than operating on a single blockchain network, trueluck multichain raffles are deployed across multiple networks while maintaining consistent execution logic.

### 1. Entry Submission

Participants connect a compatible wallet and submit their entry. The transaction is recorded on-chain, creating a permanent and tamper-proof record.

### 2. Smart Contract Execution

The raffle runs through a smart contract that governs:

* Entry validation
* Participant tracking
* Winner selection rules
* Prize allocation

Because the logic is embedded directly into the contract, no manual interference is possible once the raffle begins.

### 3. Winner Selection

When the raffle period ends, the smart contract executes its winner selection process. The result is recorded on-chain and can be independently verified by anyone.

### 4. Prize Distribution

The smart contract automatically transfers the prize according to predefined rules. Every payout transaction is publicly visible.
